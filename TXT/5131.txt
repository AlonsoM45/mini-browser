This is a well written book that is aimed at practitioners. Some of the ideas have been implemented in our inventory system (Premier Foods, UK). We've all read the theories of inventory control at some point or another, but Yang provides sound guidance towards transferring theories into practice for deteriorating products. Each suggestion is well grounded in common sense and experience. Regardless of whether you are in manufacturing, distribution or retail, you will find ideas in this book that are applicable in you business.
