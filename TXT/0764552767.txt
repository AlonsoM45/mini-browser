this book is full of valuable information for any large breed dog, but especialy for greyhounds. If you are thinking of adopting a retired racing greyhound, this is a must read. These dogs have lots of little quirks due to the way they were raised, housed, fed, and treated at the track that require some transitioning into a home.  This book provides good tips as well as the things you will need to make your hound comfortable in his/her new home.
