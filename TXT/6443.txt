If you love stories about dragons, and protectors you will love this story. Her tattoo of a dragon becomes her protector. Ronnie falls in love with Liam, and Liam falls in love with her but is it possible for them to have a relationship? It is a story based on personal experiences. It may be short but it is full of details and keeps you interested.I hope the author does a second part. Maybe we will find out if there is a way for Ronnie and Liam to have a relationship.
