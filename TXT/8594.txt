The picture book is about a kangaroo being pursued by dingoes. Will the kangaroo be able to escape the pack of hungry animals?The author, Margot Finke is from Australia. She incorporates the religious beliefs of the people from the Aboriginal culture and information about the other wildlife and the environment into the story in an entertaining way.There are footnotes on many of the pages that give more factual information which will be helpful to older kids wanting to learn more about the culture.The illustrations by Mustafa Delioglu capture the mystical and exciting feeling of the story very well.
